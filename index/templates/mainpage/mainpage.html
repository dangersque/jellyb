" Dreamy synth indie space rock from the far reaches of the outer MidWest... "

A band, guitarist, vocalist and songwriter from the lead mines of Missouri. Trapped insidie a bedroom, they managed to create the dreamy synth-driven soundscape with a mix of ambient serenity and heartwarming melodies.
They make and release music that is colorful, quirky, and alive. Sometimes it feel like I'm in a dream.
Lonely people need a chance to dance. You don't have to listen to me. You may not like what I have to say.
The brave band with a state of the art song making system.

It's a never-ending battle between the cosmic forces of good and evil, with The Nebula serving as an willing conduit for all this energy, whether they like it or not.
